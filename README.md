# NUST KSA Chapter Alumni Association Website

Site repository for the **NUST KSA Chapter Alumni Association Website**. This beta-platform serves as a central hub to connect, engage, and support National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST) alumni residing and working in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA).

## 🚀 Key Features

- **🔒 Secure Alumni Directory:** A fully searchable and filterable database of registered alumni. Users can find peers by graduation year, industry, company, and KSA region while maintaining data privacy.
- **📅 Community Events:** Stay updated on upcoming chapter meetups, professional networking nights, webinars, and annual reunions with RSVP capabilities.
- **📚 Knowledge Base Articles:** A rich repository of helpful resource guides, relocation tips for newcomers to KSA, career advancement advice, and local corporate insights.
- **📜 Chapter Charter:** Quick access to the core mission, values, constitution, and legal guidelines governing the NUST KSA Chapter.
- **🤝 Executive & Regional Contacts:** Direct communication channels and contact directories for the current Executive Council members and designated Regional Heads (Central, Western, and Eastern provinces).
